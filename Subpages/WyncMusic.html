Wynk Music is an app that is developed by a very famous and giant network operator Airtel. Wynk music has the highest number of downloads (nearly 80 million) as a music app from the Play Store in India. JioSaavan, Ganna are the main rivals for this app. Wynk provides HD voice quality songs and instant updates for newly released songs whether it is a Bollywood song or a Hollywood. It also provides various regional languages song. This app allows you to follow your favorite singer so they can ping on your favorite singer's update.
